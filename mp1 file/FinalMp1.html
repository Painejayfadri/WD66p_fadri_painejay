Our Services

The experience we accumulated in the construction industry has provided HBC,Holdings,Inc. necessary to provide in each construction services that we offer.

Consultation and Project Planning

Minimizing owner’s risk and reducing delivery schedules by overcoming the design and construction phases requires a team of construction professionals who understand how to effectively combine design, permit and construction schedules. Our design / construction team not only understands the process, they appreciate the complexities behind it and will talk to you and plan the whole thing perfectly in order to make the execution of work as smoothly as possible.

Project Management

From start to finish, our construction management group ensures that planning, coordination and control results in a project that meets your budget, deadline and quality standards. We provide pre-planning, design, construction, engineering and management guidance that delivers the best possible results.

Budget and Cost Estimation

When starting a work, the ideal is to know exactly how long the works will last and, consequently, when they will finish. Therefore, before getting your hands dirty, it is important to plan in detail the services that will be performed at all stages of project execution. This organized schedule allows the builder to buy or hire materials, labor and equipment at the right time. If he does this after the ideal moment, the work is delayed. If you do it early, you may lose materials in stock or pay for labor and equipment that ends up without work.

Design

We do architectural, interior and structural design where you’ll see your vision come alive. We design residential and construction project you would love to stay in. Not only that, we design projects to ensure that the system remains efficient, avoiding loss of quality over time.
